Gravity Jelly


===== SHORT DESCRIPTION (≤80) =====

===== FULL DESCRIPTION (≤4000) =====

Gravity Jelly is a casual block-fitting puzzle game with a signature twist you have never seen before: you can ROTATE gravity by 90° to slam the whole board exactly where you want!

🧩 GAMEPLAY
• Drop jelly pieces onto a 9×9 board and clear full rows or columns to score.
• Stuck? Rotate gravity to send ENTIRE clusters crashing to one side — opening up brand new moves.
• Clear lines back to back to trigger chain COMBOS; every big combo even refills your gravity twists.
• Merge same-colored blocks to create SUPER BLOCKS that explode and sweep the board clean.

🌏 GAME MODES
• Endless: play until you run out of room and chase your own high score.
• Campaign: journey through the Meadow, Forest and River worlds, and defeat challenging bosses.
• Leaderboard: compare your scores with players everywhere (Google Play Games).

✨ FEATURES
• Lively jelly blocks with expressive eyes and color-based personalities.
• Play offline, single player — no internet needed.
• Supports English and Vietnamese, switch language right inside the app.
• Free to play (contains ads).

Easy to learn, hard to master. Twist your mind, twist gravity — challenge your brain today!
